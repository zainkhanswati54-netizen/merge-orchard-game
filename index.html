THE ETERNAL ORCHARD

Long ago, there was a magical orchard called the Eternal Orchard.

It was the most beautiful orchard in the world — producing magical fruits whose life energy brought peace and prosperity to the entire kingdom.

THE BLIGHT

But one day, a mysterious darkness known as The Blight spread across the land.

Trees died. Fruit disappeared. Magic was lost.

The Eternal Orchard was shattered into 150 fragments. Its guardians vanished.

THE CHOSEN ONE

You are the new Orchard Keeper — the last person chosen by the ancient Tree of Life.

Your mission:
Restore the Eternal Orchard.
Awaken the Legendary Guardians.
Defeat the Blight.

Bring life back to the world.

YOUR POWER

Every merge you make generates Life Energy.

Life Energy restores the orchard — fragment by fragment, guardian by guardian.

You are not chasing points. You are saving the world.
